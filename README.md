# 🎣 Phishing Analysis

Email phishing project

## 📌 Objective
To analyze phishing email techniques. And used the analysis to provide slides used to educate the employee to better prepare for future threats.

---

## 🔍 Indicators

- Fake sender address
- Urgent language
- Malicious link
- Spelling error

---

## ⚠️ Risk

Users may unknowingly give away credentials.

---

## 🛡️ Prevention

- Verify sender identity
- Avoid clicking unknown links
- Use email filters
